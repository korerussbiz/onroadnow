Positions & Professional Conduct
🚗 Delivery Driver (car/scooter)
  • Pick up items from stores/restaurants
  • Deliver to customer within promised time
  • Check order accuracy before leaving
  • Be polite, wear clean clothes, communicate delays
🚶 On‑Foot Deliverer (walker)
  • Best for short distances (under 2 km)
  • Use a cart or backpack for small items
  • Always carry a phone with GPS
  • Greet customers with a smile
🛒 Shopper
  • Select items as per customer's list
  • Check expiry dates and quality
  • Message customer if substitution needed
🤝 Etiquette for All Roles
  • Always be respectful and punctual
  • Wear identifiable attire (onroadnow2 badge)
  • Keep customer data confidential
  • Resolve issues politely or escalate to support
