How OnRoadNow Works – Earn Money as a Deliverer

OnRoadNow connects people who need items from local stores with deliverers who can pick up and deliver them. It's a simple way to earn extra cash using your own time and vehicle.


💰 Fees & Earnings

  • Standard delivery: Buyer pays the delivery fee (set by deliverer).
  • "Deliverer Fronts Cost" – If the buyer doesn't have a card or e‑transfer, the deliverer can pay for the item upfront. The buyer reimburses the item cost + 22.4% fee upon delivery confirmation.
  • Example: Item costs JMD 1000. Deliverer fronts JMD 1000. Upon delivery, buyer pays JMD 1224 (1000 + 224 fee). Deliverer recovers JMD 1000 + JMD 224 profit.

📱 Step‑by‑Step Tutorial

1. Sign up / Change User ID
Click "Change ID" (top right) to set a unique nickname. This identifies you in the system.
2. Choose your role
In "Deliverer Tools" set your role to Customer (to request deliveries) or Deliverer (to earn money).
3. Find a store
Move the Google Map to any location worldwide. It will automatically show nearby shops, restaurants, and markets. Click any place to pre‑fill the store name.
4. Post a request (as Customer)
Fill in the item description and cost, choose payment method, and submit. Your request will appear in "Open Requests".
5. Accept a request (as Deliverer)
Browse open requests. If you accept, you agree to buy and deliver the item. For "Deliverer Fronts Cost", you also offer to pay the store upfront. After delivery, the customer confirms, and you receive the item cost + 22.4% fee.
6. Track your delivery
Deliverers can share their live location (Start Tracking). Customers can see where the deliverer is on the map (in "Your Active Deliveries").
7. Confirm delivery & get paid
When the item reaches the customer, they click "Confirm Delivery". Funds are automatically released to the deliverer (simulated). In real implementation, this would trigger a payment transfer.

OnRoadNow is operated by KorerussBiz. We charge a small platform fee (included in the 22.4% rate) to cover costs.
